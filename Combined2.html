Step-by-Step Instructions to Hit Conqueror

Step 1: Choose the Right Mode. Focus on Solo or Duo modes for faster rank climbing.

Step 2: Play Smart, Not Aggressive. Prioritize survival over kills, and stay in safe zones.

Step 3: Play at Peak Times. Rank points are higher during peak times when thereâ€™s more competition.

Step 4: Use Vehicles for Rotations. Stay mobile and avoid unnecessary fights until the endgame.

Step 5: Master the Maps. Knowing every nook and cranny of Erangel, Miramar, and Sanhok can give you the edge.

Step 6: Play with High-Skilled Teammates. Good team coordination is key in duo or squad modes.

Requirements to Hit Conqueror:

  • You need to be at least Ace rank.
  • You must be in the top 500 players.
  • You can check your rank by clicking on the Season button and then clicking on the badge of your rank.

Additional Tips:

  • Use headphones for better sound awareness.
  • Pick safe drop locations with fewer players.
  • Always carry enough healing items and grenades.
  • Track your performance and adjust strategies accordingly.
